What We Do

Our Initiatives

Transforming lives through targeted programs that create lasting impact

01 Pillar I

Capacity Building & Education

We go beyond basic schooling. Our Leadership & Career Readiness program prepares final-year secondary students for the realities of the global workforce through workshops on critical thinking.

Additionally, our Mentorship Circles connect young high-achievers with experienced professionals in Law, Tech, and Medicine for 6-month guidance cycles.

02 Pillar II

Economic Growth & Innovation

Digital Africa is our flagship tech bootcamp. We train young women and men in Frontend Development, UI/UX Design, and Digital Marketing to close the digital divide.

Graduates can apply for The Seed Fund, a micro-grant program that provides initial capital (up to ₦100,000) for youth-led startups solving local problems.

03 Pillar III

Advocacy & Outreach

Civic Voices organizes town halls where youth can engage local leaders on policy. We believe accountability starts with participation.

Our Community Drives provide targeted relief—distributing sanitary pads to keep girls in school and notebooks to rural primary schools.
